I received Training for the Uphill Athlete as a gift and found myself particularly interested in estimating my aerobic and lactate thresholds (AeT and LT, respectively). This page is probably best thought of as a journal or lab notebook where I can document my measurements and thought process, and more generally, as an outlet in which I can exercise my compulsion to do data science.

To obtain estimates of each metabolic marker, I'm mostly following the methods outlined in the book and described here. I'm also using Bayesian methods to quantify the uncertainty around these estimates, because estimates without some quantification of their uncertainty are, in my opinion, useless.
